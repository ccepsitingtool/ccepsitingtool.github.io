Usability Resources


Google Chrome

  • Increase Text Size (PC): To increase the size of text and other items on page press Ctrl and + to increase the zoom level and Ctrl and - to decrease the level of zoom.
  • Increase Text Size (Mac): To increase the size of text and other items on page press Command and + to increase the zoom level and Command and - to decrease the level of zoom.

Useful Extenstions

  • Zoom Text Only: Extension for zooming text without changing the size of images on a page.
  • Dyslexia Reader Chrome: Extension that makes text easier to read.
  • Care Your Eyes: Extension that changes a webpage's background color to reseda or night mode to protect your eyes from intensity of white or other lightness color.

  • Mobile Dyslexic: Extensions that replaces all fonts of all web pages with carefully crafted special font which is easier to read by people with dyslexia.

Internet Explorer

  • Increase Text Size: To increase the size of text and other items on page press Ctrl and + to increase the zoom level and Ctrl and - to decrease the level of zoom.
  • Zoom Tool: Open Internet Explorer, select the Tools button and then select Zoom. Select a predefined zoom level, or select a custom level by selecting Custom and entering a zoom value.
